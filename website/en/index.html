BUILT BEYOND THE BASICS

Small details that make
long conversations easy.

Call quality you can see

Check RTT, jitter, packet loss and codec, and let broken audio recover automatically.

Share only the audio you mean

On Windows you can share the audio of one selected application instead of the whole machine.

Permissions that fit the team

Split access, posting, voice, audio and screen sharing rights by role and by user.

A server you own

Run the server with nothing but Node.js 18+, and add TURN when you need external connections to hold up.

DMs outside the channel

Start a 1:1 conversation with a unique user code and keep the important context going.

Focus mode that protects the flow

Collapse the sidebars and let the chat, notes or canvas fill the screen.
